THE SEAQUEST: AN INTERACTIVE GAME TO IMPROVE THE EXPERIENCE IN THE MARINE CENTER

Fjord & Bælt is a small research and experience center opened since 1997 in Fynland in Denmark. On a first hand, Fjord & Bælt is a research center focusing on the marine animal life living in the waters of Denmark , there you can learn about seals and dolphins swimming in the fjord water. In an other hand, the center has the objective to transmit those knowledge to people. They want to improve the center and the exhibition in order to attract visitors and make them learn in a playful way, in an other way make them learn and remember the visit through games.

Our missions to help the center Fjord & Bælt, was to find a way to improve the value and user experience through a multimedia production before, during and after the visit. After a design thinking process, we came with the idea to develop an interactive game, that will make young and old alike sail through the marine center..

  • BEFORE: In order to attrack more customer, our recommandation was to redesign the website and develop a paid social media strategy (Facebook).

  • DURING: to enhave the experience inside the marine center, we decided to build a full interactive game based on the different animals present in the ecenter. The game is accessible with a tablet that a staff wil give to you at the entrace. You will be the hero of the game called "The seaquest".

  • AFTER: To push the visitor to come back we decided to create some additional material, such as a diploma as soon as kids will complete the SeaQuest game, and a badge. Moreover, and through the ipad, we create a formular to ask about feedback regarding the center, and also collecting email for futur newsletter.
