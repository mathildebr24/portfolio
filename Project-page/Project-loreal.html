L'Oréal: Brand design

During my year of apprenticeship at L'Oréal Group's Research & Innovation department, I accompanied an artistic director on the "Sharing Day 2019" , the annual event of the R&I. I have worked on the creation of a hundreds of info-settings, designed to illustrate Vice President Laurent Attal's annual speeches. This event gather about to 2500 L'Oréal collaborators at the Paris Event Center in Porte de la Villette.

My missions during my apprenticeship:

  • Development of communication tools and supports (powerpoint, logotype, mapping) to contribute to the evolution of the "R&I 360" organization

  • Brand content promote teams and employees expertises (filming, editing, design of strategic messages, screen design, selection of rushs, validation of assemblies)

  • Artistic direction for the creation of the info-setting for the speeches of the Vice-President of Research
