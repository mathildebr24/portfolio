TRAVELING IN ROMANIA: THE BUS LOVE-STORY

Travelling to Romania is similar to most European countries: train, plane, bus, cab and uber. However, among those means, Romanians seem to prefer one in particular: the bus.

And there are several reasons for this:

  • The under developed train infrastructure: : These lines are indeed quite old, and to get from point A to point B, the train often takes as much time as the bus or the car.

  • It's cheap: Traveling by bus is a cheap way to travel across romania.

  • Lack of digitalization: The Romanian bus companies (usually) do not sell online their ticket, so you have to go directly to the station, or in the bus. For many Romanians, and especially the older generations which is not necessarely up to date with digital skills, this is the easiest way for them to travel across the country

  • Sending parcels: It is possible to drop a parcel in a bus, so that another person can pick it up once the bus arrives at destination, or when the bus stops in several cities on its way.

The objective of the mobile application La drum (which is a romanian experssion to say: let's go) is to be able to make bus offers available in one centralized space, whether to aquire tickets for a trip, or to send a package via bus lines, LaDrum enables you to get the right offer for you.
