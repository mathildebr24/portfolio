THE LEASING: A NEW SERVICE IN COHERENCE WITH THE BRAND PURPOSE

Maximum is a furniture company.Their objective is simple: to offer high quality furniture, made in France and entirely "recycled", and all at an affordable price. It is during a school competition that Maximum came to propose us to work on solutions to invent a new service related to their branded DNA. After a week's work, my team and I came up with a new service based on leasing .

THE BIG IDEA

  • Enable the brand to establish a lasting relationship with individuals

  • Extends the life cycle of materials by allowing the company to use products that have been leased and are no longer in use

  • Tells a story about the furniture ("one of the 100 chairs that was produced for such and such an event for such and such a brand")

  • Allows the consumer to live an experience that raises awareness of the issue dear to Maximum: the reduction of waste

  • Allows the consumer to be an actor in the life cycle of materials

  • Allows the consumer to benefit from a lower cost price

  • Allows the consumer to be able to change furniture regularly

WHAT IS THE MAXIMUM ALTERNATIVE?

Maximum furniture has many lives. You can buy them new, or rent our furniture that has already had a previous life. At term, you can take advantage of the purchase option, we will return the furniture in question to make it happy again!
