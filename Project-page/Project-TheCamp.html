The Camp: Graphic & Brand Design

The Camp, is a co-working space in Copenhagen, brought by Tryg & Talent Garden Rainmaking. The Camp is Tryg's co-working space for startups and is a collaboration between Tryg, an experienced corporate player with 4 million insurance customers in Scandinavia, and Talent Garden Rainmaking, one of Europe's leading facilitators of entrepreneurship.

During my internship, I was in charge to work on the communication for the Camp's community.
In other words, my missions was:

  • Intern communication: create any kind of support to share co-working space news

  • Brand content: such as visual for Linkeding and Facebook, poster and kakemono for events

  • Print: create magazine to promote The Camp during events

  • Event supports
