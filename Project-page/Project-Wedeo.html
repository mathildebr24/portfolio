Project Thesis

Wedeo : A new online platform to build your video in 3 steps

During my work-study year at L'Oréal, and in order to validate my master's degree, I was given the task of creating a project that would respond to my company issue : To give a voice to the collaborator and, to promote the use of video

I went on a needs analysis within the group's Research & Innovation department, integrating the recurring requests made to the communication team. From this research, I highlighted the issue of creating video content.

Although video is used and known by everyone, it is rarely used by employees. Indeed, for many of them, it is often complex and delicate to create content to share their projects, innovations, either because of a lack of time or a lack of knowledge of creation tools. After a surey, I found out that employees expect new formats and simpler access to information .

BUT, at the same time, employees are scared to speak up...

The paradoxe : "The collaborators know how to use tools for filming and are used to social networks but they do not feel legitimate to become authors of video content."

How can we promote the use of video to create content when the people who should use it to deliver relevant information don't feel legitimate?

MY SOLUTION: THE ONLINE TOOLKIT VIDEO

I've created a prototype that will answer this problem of creating video content. To do this I first developed a questionnaire, then from the results I made several sketches to arrive at a wireframe and finally a mockup.

  • First step: write your project detail
  • Seconde step: select the type of video you want to build and check the process
  • Third step: finally, download your own toolkit and build your video
