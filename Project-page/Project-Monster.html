Monster Family: Graphic Design

The Monster Family was a school project. The objective of this exercise was to create a serie of character with 6 different expressions. This can be inspired by the new version of emojis, the «stickers» that can be found on social networks today.   Finally, the last requirement was to be developed a creative and expressive «graphic universe» specific to our characters
