REDESIGN THE EXPERIENCE OF HEARING DEVICES WITH A MOBILE APP

As a hearing-impaired person, I discovered the complexities of living with hearing aids. However, to make our daily life easier, companies are developing tools to help us live better with our hearing loss, especially thanks to mobile applications.

Although the application provides a lot of functionality (volume control, creation of specific programs depending on where you are, management of high and low sounds, location of the hearing aids, etc.), I found that the application's navigation wasn't really intuitive.

Here is what I redesigned:

  • Hearing devices connection: : Claryfing the steps to connect our hearing devices to the mobile app

  • Program dashboard: Reorganisation of the hearing programs

  • SoundSense Learn: Redesign of the SoundSense Learn process
